Steak
A La Carte

These fluffy pancakes are a breakfast classic, quick and easy to make with just a few simple ingredients. Light and airy, they pair perfectly with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or your favorite toppings. Enjoy a delicious homemade breakfast thats sure to please everyone!

Ingredients:
  • 1 boneless ribeye or sirloin steak (about 1-inch thick)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (or any high smoke-point oil)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 large egg
  • Fresh herbs (like thyme or rosemary)
Instructions: Prepare the Steak: Take the steak out of the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes. This helps it cook more evenly. Season the Steak: Generously season both sides of the steak with salt and black pepper. Heat the Pan: In a large cast-iron skillet or heavy frying pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat until its shimmering but not smoking. Sear the Steak: Carefully place the steak in the hot pan. Sear for about 4-5 minutes without moving it, until a nice crust forms. Flip and Add Butter: Flip the steak using tongs. Add the butter, crushed garlic, and herbs (if using) to the pan. As the butter melts, tilt the pan slightly and spoon the melted butter over the steak for extra flavor. Cook to Desired Doneness: Continue cooking for another 3-5 minutes, depending on your preferred doneness: Rare: 120�F (49�C) Medium Rare: 130�F (54�C) Medium: 140�F (60�C) Medium Well: 150�F (66�C) Well Done: 160�F (71�C) Rest the Steak: Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest on a cutting board for about 5-10 minutes. This helps the juices redistribute. Slice and Serve: Slice against the grain and serve with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
